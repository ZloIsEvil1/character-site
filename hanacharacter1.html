Backstory

Raised in the frigid north, Bethen worked among the diasporate strength of the weave. Working to help keep her friends and family safe with what few wards and conjurations could make due in the weaker environments. The cold was the biggest drain on her strength and most of the time she worked merely on helping keep fires lit even when fuel was sparse.

Unfortunately, the magic at home burnt up. She had no idea that one could exhaust a place like that, but she had. Her home refused to answer to her spells and thus she had to leave. Had to learn as quickly as possible. The kind of learning she would need is not the kind that comes easily to those of the north, thus she went south. To the greater cities, to learn amongs the mages and other arcanely inclined.
